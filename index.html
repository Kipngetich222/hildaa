COURSE CONTENT

Organic chemistry is the study of the structure,properties,composition,reactions,and preparations of carbon- containing compounds.Most organic compounds contain carbon and hydrogen,but they may also include any number of other elements (e.g,nitrogen,oxygen,halogens,phosphorus,siicon,sulfur)
